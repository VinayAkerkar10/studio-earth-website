About

Design Rooted in Simplicity, Sustainability, and Cultural Memory

Studio Earth creates architecture and interiors that are quiet in expression, generous in light, and grounded in how people truly live. Our work values craftsmanship, natural materials, and spaces that feel emotionally resonant without excess.

Inspired by projects like House in Bambolim, we bring together coastal sensitivity, regional character, and modern minimalism to design places that are functional, sustainable, and deeply connected to their setting.
